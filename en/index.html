Protection against pirated modules

Before purchasing a module online, make sure that the website where you intend to make the payment is not listed in the pirate resources database for Opencart.

If it is listed, we do not recommend using such modules, as all of this can lead to a lack of technical support, compromise the security of your website, infect it with malicious code, leak personal data and payment information of your customers, and cause other problems.
